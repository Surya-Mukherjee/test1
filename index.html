Cattle & Buffalo Breed Identification App
AI application for instant and real-time identification of cattle breeds on the get go.

Introduction

Empowering farmers to make informed breeding decisions. Our platform simplifies livestock management with real-time insights. Discover smarter ways to enhance productivity every day.

What we used?

The platform uses machine learning algorithms on various Indian buffalo and cattle datasets. It inspects images, regardless of the conditions, to provide the probable breed. Outputs are validated against a breed repository and may be interfaced with the Breed Population Analytics (BPA) system for on-time registration checking.

Benefits of our site

  • Quickly tells you the breed of your cattle or buffalo with just a photo.
  • Saves your time and makes farming easier.
  • Gives you useful information about each breed instantly.
  • Simple and easy to use for everyone.
